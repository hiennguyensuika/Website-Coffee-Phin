About us - Our Mission


Imagine it's 6 o'clock in the morning, you open the window, take a deep fresh air breath. After that, like usual, you walk down to your kitchen, have yourself a cup of water, you feel recharged after a very tight sleep. Now your mind is so clear with freshness, you come closer to the drawer and take out a phin which is a beautiful shining Vietnamese coffee phin that your friend gave you as your birthday gift.

As a Saigonese, this coffee phin means not only for the taste of the coffee, but to you its the taste of Vietnam, your beloved homecountry. Although, you are living further away from home, but whenever seeing the image of this phin, it makes you feel home.

Thats the mission Hello Phins is carrying and doing our best to deliver it to you. We create the best carry-on Vietnamese coffee phin package, which you can bring it along to where-ever and whenever you want. No matter where you are, everytime you look besides, there is always a cup of Vietnamese coffee staying aside with you.
